## In-game rendering (procedural)
Hex grid, glowing snake segments, food orbs, and arena ring are drawn in SpriteKit to match the slither.io aesthetic without relying on generated placeholder art.

Download manually (site requires browser):
- [Kenney UI Pack](https://kenney.nl/assets/ui-pack) — CC0 buttons, panels, icons
